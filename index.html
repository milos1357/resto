About

Welcome to Resto

The Resto Restaurant is one of the top restaurants in Italy - just ask our regular customers. We combine our own family recipes with style and personal flair to create a unique experience every time you visit us. We strive to make each visit satisfying and memorable.

The Resto Restaurant has continually provided each of its guests with only the finest in Mexican cuisine since 2003. The Resto specializes in creating a uniquely thoughtful atmosphere, which will not only excite one's tastes, but provide the perfect dining experience.

Our restaurant, which used to be The Pony Post back "in the day", is located near the borders of Central Park and features dishes ranging from burgers, nachos and chicken to an assortment of appetizers, pastas and fresh salads.
